B.E. in Computer Science

Hi! I'm Santiago Góngora. I got my B.E. in Computer Science from Universidad de la República (Uruguay), where I'm also a member of its NLP research group (Grupo de Procesamiento de Lenguaje natural).

My Bachelor thesis was about Guaraní, a low-resource language spoken in South america (mostly Paraguay), advised by Luis Chiruzzo.

In the future I would like to focus my research on computational creativity and automated story generation, as well as continuing my work on low-resource languages.
